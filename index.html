Storms in Australia

Storms in Australia can cause significant harm, with around 350
storms per year being considered severe, & 1 to 2 of these
causing recorded injury.

Weather extremes

The map on the right considers the extreme values of an average
year. Weather at each major city is averaged from 1980 to 2010.
Then the most extreme (max/min) value is noted.

Reporting bias

Note that the storm data is biased towards populated regions,
as storms are reported rather than recorded on sensors. This
is reflected in the weather map by only plotting at major cities.
